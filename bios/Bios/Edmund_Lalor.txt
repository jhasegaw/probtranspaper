University College Dublin in 1998 and 2007 and the M.Sc. degree in

school teacher for children with learning difficulties, Ed joined
MIT's Media Lab Europe, where he worked from 2002-2005 investigating
brain-computer interfacing and attentional mechanisms in the
brain. He then spent 2 years as a postdoctoral researcher at the
Nathan Kline Institute for Psychiatric Research and 2 years as a
Government of Ireland Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Trinity College
Dublin. Following a brief stint at University College London, he
returned to Trinity College Dublin as an Assistant Professor in
2011. In 2016, he joined the Departments of Biomedical Engineering and
Neuroscience at the University of Rochester as an Associate Professor.
