He is Professor of ECE at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, Full-Time Faculty in the Beckman Institute for Advanced Science and Technology, and Affiliate Professor in the Departments of Speech and Hearing Science, Computer Science, and Linguistics.  He is currently a member of the IEEE SLTC, an Associate Editor of JASA, Treasurer of ISCA, Secretary of SProSIG, and ISCA Liaison for SIGML.  He is author or co-author of 51 journal articles, 148 conference papers, 43 printed abstracts, and 5 US patents.  His primary research areas are in the application of phonological and psychophysical concepts to audio and audiovisual speech recognition and synthesis, to multimedia analytics, and to the creation of speech technology in under-resourced languages.
